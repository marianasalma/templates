The Influencer
Kepribadian si Creator-Mentor
Description
THE INFLUENCERs excel in fostering creativity and innovation within their organizations or industries. They prioritize building strong relationships and creating a culture of experimentation and artistic expression. They excel not just in creating but in nurturing a community of like-minded individuals, fostering a culture of continuous learning and artistic expression.

Examples of Entrepreneur: Alice Waters (Chef, Restaurateur): As the founder of Chez Panisse, Alice Waters is a pioneer in the farm-to-table movement. Beyond her culinary skills, she guides others by advocating for sustainable and organic practices in the food industry.
Strength
Nurtures creativity, able to understand their industry and consistent in providing output
Weakness
Resistant to change and adopt new trends, lack of emphasis on strategic direction
Opportunity
Potential to open for nurturing the next generation talents and attracts them
Threats
Rapid industry shifts cause changes, risk of stagnation without strategic vision
