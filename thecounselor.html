The Counselor
Kepribadian si Connector-Mentor
Description
THE COUNSELORs excel in mentoring and supporting others on their personal and professional journeys. They prioritize fostering deep connections and providing guidance and encouragement to those they mentor. However, they may struggle with setting clear boundaries between work and personal life, which could lead to burnout.

Examples of Entrepreneur: Jack Ma (Alibaba Group): known for his delegation skills and fostering a culture of collaboration at Alibaba by assembling a diverse team with complementary skills.
Strength
Strong mentorship skills, maintaining a supportive and encouraging environment
Weakness
Struggle with setting clear work-life boundaries
Opportunity
Potential for fostering growth and development within individuals
Threats
Risk of burnout due to blurred work-life boundaries
