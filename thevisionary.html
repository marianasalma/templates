The Visionary
Kepribadian si Innovator-Mentor
Description
THE VISIONARY is an entrepreneur who excels in providing strategic guidance and foresight to their teams or clients. They prioritize creating clear visions for the future and navigating complex challenges with confidence. They are not just industry experts; they are forward-thinking visionaries who leverage their accumulated knowledge to drive innovation.

Examples of Entrepreneur: Warren Buffett (Berkshire Hathaway): Renowned for his investment wisdom, Buffett's strategic decisions and long-term vision make him a visionary investor.
Strength
Possesses extensive knowledge and experience in their industry, able to foresee trends and make informed decisions
Weakness
Hesitant to adopt radical changes due to a reliance on proven methods, Tendency to overanalyze situations, potentially delaying decision-making
Opportunity
Becomes a respected thought leader in the industry, forming alliances based on industry insights, potential for leading transformational projects and initiatives
Threats
Rapid industry shifts might challenge established methods and insights. Lack of strong interpersonal connections may hinder team dynamics
