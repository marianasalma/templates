The Mentor
Kepribadian si Mentor-Connector
Description
THE MENTORs excel in fostering collaboration and synergy within their teams or organizations. They prioritize building strong relationships and creating a culture of teamwork and cooperation. However, they may need to focus on developing their strategic vision and decision-making skills to ensure long-term success.

Examples of Entrepreneur: Reid Hoffman (LinkedIn): He actively mentors young entrepreneurs and advocates for fostering a network of support to guide younger generations.
Strength
Strong team-building and collaboration skills with Positive team culture
Weakness
Lack of emphasis on strategic vision and decision making skills
Opportunity
Potential for achieving team goals more effectively
Threats
Risk of stagnation without any innovation and strategic direction
