The Wizard
Kepribadian si Innovator-Connector
Description
THE ENFORCER is a dynamic entrepreneur who excels in building and leveraging networks for success. They prioritize building strong relationships and creating mutually beneficial partnerships. Their strength lies not only in their innovative ideas but also in the strategic alliances they forge.

Examples of Entrepreneur: Richard Branson (Virgin Group): Known for his extensive network and ability to form strategic partnerships across various industries, from music to airlines.
Strength
Strong networking and relationship-building skills, fostering collaborations and partnerships
Weakness
Heavily relies on the strength of their networks, Lack of emphasis on strategic direction
Opportunity
Forming partnerships to drive innovation, becoming a key influencer and thought leader
Threats
Dependence on networks may become a weakness if connections weaken
